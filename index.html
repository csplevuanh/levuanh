Hello - to someone reading this! I'm a rising senior at Beloit College, pursuing a B.Sc. in Mathematics. As an MSRP intern at MIT, I work with Prof. Haruko Murakami Wainwright in the Department of Nuclear Science and Engineering, developing machine learning models for environmental monitoring as part of the Department of Energy's ALTEMIS project. I’m also designing a physics-informed neural network framework to solve Partial Differential Equations efficiently. Post-graduation, I plan to pursue a Ph.D. in scientific computing, potentially at an applied mathematics or computational science department. On this website, you can find my CV or resume attached on the second page.
