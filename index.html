Hello - to someone reading this! I'm a rising senior at Beloit College, pursuing a B.Sc. in Mathematics. I am currently designing a mathematics-driven framework to analyze the behaviors of different neural network classes at Google Research. During the MSRP 2024 intern at MIT, I worked with Prof. Haruko Murakami Wainwright in the Department of Nuclear Science and Engineering, developing machine learning algorithms for applications in computational chemistry. Post-graduation, he plans to pursue doctoral studies in the foundations of machine learning, geometric and algebraic methods, and algorithmic design. On this website, you can find my CV or resume attached on the second page.
