I develop geometric and topological methods for solving computational problems. By deriving techniques from information geometry, spectral analysis, and topological data analysis, I prove theoretical guarantees and design tools for data representation, machine learning, and autoformalization.

1. Data Representation

I study spectral and topological invariants, e.g., Laplacian spectra, diffusion operators, and persistent homology, to capture geometry at multiple scales. The goal is representation schemes that are stable under noise and deformations while remaining expressive for downstream tasks.
