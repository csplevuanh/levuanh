  • Research Associate @VinCyber

Hello! I'm a researcher with a strong interest in algorithm design and analysis, focusing on quantum computing. As a research associate at the VinUni Cybersecurity Lab, I work on quantum-resistant algorithms and next-generation cryptographic protocols. I am also open to other topics in quantum information science, lightweight implementations of quantum technologies, and operations research in quantum systems and other domains.

I hold a Bachelor of Science in Mathematics from Beloit College. I have interned at Google Research and MIT. You can find my CV or resume on this page.
