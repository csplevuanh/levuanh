I am looking for Ph.D. opportunities in data‑centric geometric AI and remote sensing! My work bridges geometric machine learning, applied topology & geometry, and remote‑sensing data analytics. I currently serve as a Researcher at the Data Science and Applications Lab, Institute of Information Technology, Vietnam Academy of Science and Technology (supervised by Prof. Nguyen Viet Anh and Prof. Nguyen Truong Thang). I also collaborate with Prof. Paul Liang at MIT EECS’s Multisensory Intelligence Group. My prior experiences include research internships at Google Research, the University of Tokyo, and Beloit College. Below I present three research thrusts with representative work and future plans.

1. Data Preprocessing & Geometric Cleaning

Accurate, geometry‑aware preprocessing is the key to reliable insights. My ICML 2025 DIG‑BUGS paper on RN‑F contamination mitigation models noise as a geometric distortion and removes corrupted samples in large language models. Prior work in Topology‑Preserving Scaling and Stability of Persistence Diagrams under Non‑Uniform Scaling provides mathematical guarantees that transformations respect intrinsic structure. I am extending these results to multimodal (vision‑text‑SAR) pipelines with anomaly detection.

2. Mathematics of Data & Model Dynamics

I study how data propagate through neural operators. Results in How Analysis Can Teach Us the Optimal Way to Design Neural Operators and the forthcoming Mathematical Analysis of Neural Operator Behaviors connect functional analysis with architecture design. Current preprints on Lipschitz bounds for persistent Laplacians and Spectral contraction on δ‑hyperbolic graphs extend these ideas to graph signal processing and guarantee robustness.

3. Remote‑Sensing & Environmental Analytics

My 2024 study in the International Journal of Environmental Studies achieved millimetre‑level land‑subsidence mapping from multi‑temporal SAR by combining geometric filtering with PSI. As an AI advisor at GeoHub.ai I design physics‑informed pipelines for automated environmental monitoring. I am pursuing hybrid physics‑data models for climate risk and natural‑hazard forecasting across ASEAN.
