Hello - to someone reading this! I'm a rising senior at Beloit College, pursuing a B.Sc. in Mathematics. I am currently designing a mathematics-driven framework to analyze the behaviors of different neural network classes at Google Research. During the MSRP 2024 intern at MIT, I worked with Prof. Haruko Murakami Wainwright in the Department of Nuclear Science and Engineering, developing machine learning algorithms for applications in computational chemistry. Post-graduation, he plans to pursue a Ph.D. in theoretical computer science and explore its applications in autonomous scientific discovery. On this website, you can find my CV or resume attached on the second page.
